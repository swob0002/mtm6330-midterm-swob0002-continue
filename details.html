Details

Mark and Marie are getting married!

Please join us at Sharbot Lake on this special day. We'll be hosting a casual wedding focused on celebrating this milestone with those closest to us. High-level details:

  • Dress code is casual. There's a lake and you're welcome to go swimming, boating, whatever you'd like. Be comfortable and enjoy yourself.
  • The ceremony part will be non-religious and brief. We want to have a good time instead of trying to impress a higher power.
  • A variety of accommodations (details below) are available nearby, and it's about a 1.5 hour drive to downtown Ottawa.

Questions or comments for both us can be sent to marieandmark@swoboda.ca. You can also use whatever your preferred contact channel is to reach either of us independently.

Agenda

  • 2 to 4 PM: Arrive and mingle, swim, run naked through the woods and embrace your inner sasquatch, what have you.
  • 4 to 4:30 PM: Wedding ceremony with some words from friends.
  • 4:30 to 5 PM: More free time while dinner is setup.
  • 5 to 6:30 PM: Dinner. Accommodations will be available for vegetarians, vegans, gluten-free. Let us know your dietary restrictions.
  • 6:30 PM onward: Adult beverages, dancing, swimming, feats of strength, and other forms of merriment. Arrive between 2 to 4 PM, head out after dinner when it suits you.

Children

We love your children but are aiming for more of a grown-up party. Consider this an invitation to take a night off and have grown-up fun with us. Let us know if this will be a challenge for you.

Accommodations

There are a number of options for guests to stay nearby. We've reserved some rooms for guests which should be booked ASAP to ensure you have a place to stay: Mohawk Lodge (up to 50 people on Saturday onward): This family resort area is a 10 minute walk away and has 11 cottages and 2 apartments. It has very affordable weekend rates, and that's a great way to spend your weekend without having to travel in and out on the day. Great if you're attending with friends or a family. Sharbot Lake Country Inn (5 rooms reserved): This local inn is a 6 minute drive away and has 7 charming rooms. It's located in Sharbot Lake itself and on top of a quaint pub. Sharbot Lake Provincial Park: For the outdoorsy types, there's a great provincial park not far on the lake. If you like camping, then this is a great and extremely low-cost option. Looking for something else? Contact us and we'll point you to something that meets your needs.

Want to drink and not worry about driving? Check the rideshare sign-up. Staying at the inn or the park? Let us know and we can see about getting you driven or water-taxied.
